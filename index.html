About me

Hello, I'm Ruben! I am currently the Head of Development of CAD4TB, an artificial intelligence software to detect tuberculosis on chest x-rays. CAD4TB is deployed in 40+ countries and screens over 7 million people. I lead a small multidisciplinary team of deep learning engineers and software engineers that focus on developing deep learning algorithms that need to fit on limited hardware. Before this, I have been a freelance project manager & web developer for multiple years with a wide portfolio of clients.
