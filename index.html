About me

Hello, I'm Ruben! A product manager with a strong technical foundation in AI and a focus on advancing healthcare technology across Europe. Currently, as product manager for IQVIA's NLP team, I'm scaling NLP solutions from the Netherlands to EMEA and implementing an outcome-centric product strategy to drive measurable impact. Previously, I led research and development for Thirona's CAD4TB, an AI-powered tool that screens for tuberculosis in 45 countries, endorsed by the WHO. My approach blends strategic vision with hands-on expertise, enabling me to deliver scalable, impactful healthcare solutions.
